Hi, my name is Jack Meyer I'm currently a Software Engineering student and a future Software Developer. I am a motivated, passionate, and outgoing student at Iowa State University. I'm in my third year and I'm majoring in Software Engineering. My current interests are in application development, especially within the healthcare industry.

I grew up in New Prague, Minnesota; a town of about 7,500 people. My childhood was spent on the basketball court and in the woods at my cabin in Northern Minnesota. I attended New Prague High School, where I became even more involved in the basketball program. My last two years I was a Student Assistant Coach on the team. Coaching taught me how to be a leader and an effective communicator. During high school I also challenged myself in the classroom by taking AP courses and college courses which were offered through a college in the school program. By challenging myself in high school and being active in extracurricular activities, I learned I graduated high school in June 2014 and I started attending Iowa State University in the fall of that year.

At Iowa State University, I chose to major in Software Engineering. As I started my classwork, I fell in love with developing software. I enjoy critically thinking and became addicted to the feeling of success after solving a difficult problem.

This past summer, I had an amazing experience at my internship. I was a Programmer Analyst Intern at Telligen in West Des Moines, Iowa. I worked on the CMS Measure Authoring Tool, implementing new features which will be included in future releases!

Today, I am very active within the Software Engineering Program. This upcoming year I will be serving as president of the Computer Science and Software Engineering Club at Iowa State and a Peer Mentor for the Software Engineering Learning Community. I am extremely excited to have the opportunity to reach out to so many students, especially freshmen, through the Computer Science and Software Engineering Club and the Learning Community. I hope to pass along my passion and excitement for the field of software engineering.
